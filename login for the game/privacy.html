Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Tagu-Taguan game (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address to authenticate your account.
  • Game Data: We track your scores, difficulty level, progress, and in-game actions to personalize your experience.
  • Device & Log Information: Basic browser/device info may be collected for analytics and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the game experience
  • To save and sync your progress across devices
  • To analyze gameplay patterns and improve our features
  • To contact you only if necessary (e.g., support-related)

3. How We Store & Protect Data

Your information is securely stored using our backend service (Supabase). All sensitive data is encrypted, and we use role-based access to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Your data is only shared with trusted services we use to host and support the game.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request to view, edit, or delete your data at any time
  • You may delete your account and all associated data
  • To make any requests, please reach out to us through our contact page

6. Cookies

We may use cookies (small local storage entries) to keep you logged in and maintain your game settings or preferences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the date at the top. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the new policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@tagu-taguan.com.
