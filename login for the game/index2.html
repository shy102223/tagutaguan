About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate developers and designers dedicated to reimagining classic Filipino games through modern digital platforms. Inspired by the joy and simplicity of traditional childhood experiences, our team aims to bring timeless games like Tagu-Taguan into today’s technology-driven world. With backgrounds in web development, game design, and user experience, we work collaboratively to create meaningful, fun, and interactive digital experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve cultural heritage through accessible and engaging online games. By transforming traditional Filipino games into digital formats, we hope to reconnect players with their roots while offering fun, educational gameplay for the new generation.

Why We Created Tagu-Taguan

With the growing preference for indoor and digital entertainment, opportunities for playing outdoor games like hide-and-seek have become limited. Tagu-Taguan was developed to bring the same excitement and challenge into a digital format. We designed the game to be more than just fun—it encourages critical thinking, attention to detail, and provides a nostalgic yet fresh experience for players of all ages.

Our Vision

We envision a series of web-based games that preserve and celebrate Filipino culture in an innovative way. Tagu-Taguan is just the beginning of our journey to build a library of games that bring tradition and technology together in a creative, playful space.
